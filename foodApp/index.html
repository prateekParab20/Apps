Guide & Tips

Why higher protein?
At 55-60 years, the body needs more protein (1.2–1.5 g/kg) to maintain muscle and strength.

Best low-GI Indian choices:
• Millets: jowar, bajra, ragi, foxtail
• Barley (jau), whole dals, moong, chana
• Lots of non-starchy sabzi (lauki, bhindi, palak, methi)
• Paneer, soya, hung curd, sprouts

Simple rules:
1. Eat protein + veggies first, then carbs
2. Use minimal oil / non-stick
3. Add lemon & spices for taste without calories
4. Stay consistent – small daily wins matter most

This is not medical advice. Please consult her doctor if she has diabetes, kidney issues or any health concerns.
