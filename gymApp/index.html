Strong & Lean Tips

Science-backed advice tailored for your goals

Fat Loss Fundamentals
  • 01 Calorie deficit: Eat 300–500 calories below maintenance. Track consistently.
  • 02 Protein priority: 1.8–2.2g per kg bodyweight → ~120–140g/day at 65kg. Keeps muscle while you lose fat.
  • 03 Strength training + steps: This program + 8–10k steps daily is incredibly effective.
  • 04 Sleep 7.5–9 hrs: Poor sleep kills fat loss and recovery.
Getting Stronger
  • 01 Progressive overload: Add weight or reps when you hit the top of the range with good form.
  • 02 Form first: Never sacrifice technique for heavier weight. Film yourself occasionally.
  • 03 Rest & recover: 90–120s between sets is perfect for this goal. Don't rush.
  • 04 Consistency beats intensity: 3 solid sessions/week every week > random hard workouts.
